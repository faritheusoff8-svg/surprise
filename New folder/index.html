To You

To my favourite person,

I know I don't always say everything I feel, sometimes the right words just don't come out when I want them to. So I thought I'd write it down instead.

This past year with you has been really good. Not perfect, because nothing is, but genuinely good. The kind of good where I catch myself smiling when I think about random moments with you. Like that time we laughed so hard over something so stupid, or the quiet evenings when we just existed together without needing to fill the silence.

I like who I am when I'm with you. You make the ordinary feel special somehow, and I don't think you even realize it.

So here's to another year. I'm looking forward to all the small moments, the late night conversations, the inside jokes, the comfortable silences, and everything in between.

Thank you for being you. I'm really glad you're in my life.

01

The way you laugh

That real laugh, not the polite one you give out of habit, but the real laugh, the one that escapes you without thinking, the one that makes your whole face light up

02

You actually listen

You don't just hear me, you remember me. The little things I mention casually, the things I don't expect you to notice but you did. And that makes me feel seen in a way I didn't know I needed.

03

Being weird with you is easy

I don't have to filter myself around you. We can be absolute idiots together and it never feels embarrassing. I can be quiet, silly, emotional, or completely unserious, and it never feels wrong.

04

You feel like home

I don't know how else to describe it. It's not about a place or a moment. The feeling that no matter how loud the world gets, being close to you makes things feel right again.

05

You're genuinely kind

Not performatively kind. Actually kind. The way you treat people, even when no one's watching, it says a lot about who you are. The way you care without expecting anything back tells me so much about your heart. It's the one I admire most about you.
